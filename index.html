Clinical Psychologist Practicing in Midrand, Johannesburg

Areas of work include psychotherapy and medico-legal assessment.

Matsedeso has achieved a BA in Sociology and Psychology (UP), a BSocSci Honours in Psychology (UP), an MA in Clinical Psychology (NMU), and is registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA), including the Board of Healthcare Funders.

With regard to psychotherapy, Matsedeso is driven by the Ubuntu values in her practice and applies an integrative therapeutic approach, which is tailored specifically to each client and their particular difficulty. However, in keeping with her aim to make therapy strategic and effective, her work most often employs the principles of solution-focused therapy (SFT), dialectic-behavioral therapy (DBT) and cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT).

Matsedeso has considerable experience in psychological assessment (including clinical diagnosis, emotional assessment, intellectual assessment, career assessment, neuropsychological assessment and medico-legal assessment) with children, adolescents and adults.
