Psychotherapy

Matsedeso uses an integrative psychotherapeutic approach in order to meet the needs of each unique person. She draws on elements of dialectic-behavioral therapy, cognitive-behavioural, solution-focused and person-centred therapies with the goal of making therapy focused, brief and effective. Psychotherapy is beneficial for various difficulties, including:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety, stress, burnout
  • Weight-loss
  • Coping skills
  • Interpersonal difficulties
  • Trauma counselling
  • Self-esteem issues
  • Anger management
  • Bereavement/grief counselling
  • Adjustment to new life circumstances
  • Behavioral issues
  • Relationship challenges
  • Pre and post-natal depression or difficulties
  • Parenting
  • Self discovery, self awareness, self acceptance
